1. Basic Analysis:

a. How many different ticket classes are there in the dataset?
b. What is the total number of passengers listed in the dataset?


2. Survival Analysis:

a. How many passengers survived, and how many did not?
b. What percentage of passengers survived?

3. Gender Analysis:

a. How many male and female passengers were on board?
b. Compare the number of male survivors to female survivors. Who had a higher survival rate?

4. Age Analysis:

a. What is the average age of the passengers?
b. How many passengers were under 18 years old?

5.Fare Analysis:

What is the range of ticket prices? (Find the minimum and maximum ticket prices)
How many passengers paid more than $50 for their ticket?

6. Family Analysis:

How many passengers traveled alone (i.e., with no siblings/spouses and no parents/children)?
What is the average number of siblings or spouses aboard for the passengers?
